The Best Movies According to Bailey

My top 3 movies of all time

Good Will Hunting

This movie is an absolute masterpiece. A touching tale of a gifted human.

Interstellar

This intergalactic movie is an epic film that defies the laws of the universe and challenges you to comprehend the impossible.

Creed

This film follows the Rocky series, and introduces you to Adonnis Creed, the son of fallen hero Apollo Creed.
